Class Image

Image

Must be one of the following values: background - The image displayed as the background of the front of the pass. footer - The image displayed on the front of the pass near the barcode. icon - The passâ€™s icon. This is displayed in notifications and in emails that have a pass attached, and on the lock screen. logo - The image displayed on the front of the pass in the top left. strip - The image displayed behind the primary fields on the front of the pass. thumbnail - An additional image displayed on the front of the pass. For example, on a membership card, the thumbnail could be used to a picture of the cardholder.
